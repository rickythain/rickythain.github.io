TOP TEN FAST FOOD

Here is a list of top ten fast food! Get to know their taste, where they came from, the best item in their menu, and find out their nearest stores to you!

Published on: 23 February 2021

Photo: Subway

1. Subway

Sandwich

Subway is founded in the United States of America. They provide an array of sandwiches with various kinds of breads, vegetables, and sauces to put into your sandwich. It is customizable in terms of its bread, vegetables, and sauces to cater to your own personal liking! Meatball Marinara is arguably the best sandwich to choose!

Find the nearest Subway

Photo: Restaurant Brands International Inc.

2. Popeyes

Chicken

Popeyes is originated from Louisiana, United States. Their chicken is the top food in their menu. With its cruncy exterior and tender white meat within, nobody could resist!

Find the nearest Popeyes

3. Shake Shack

Burger

Shake Shack was first built in New York, United States. Their signature Shack burger popular with its juicy beef is the go-to for first timers and is definitely the best item in their menu.

Find the nearest Shake Shack

Photo: Jersey Mike's

4. Jersey Mike's

Sandwich

Jersey Mike's originated from New Jersey, United States. It is well known for their sandwiches. The vegetables are fresh and their breads are baked daily to maintain the freshness and quality of the ingredients. Their best subs to eat are the turkey and the provolone.

Find the nearest Jersey Mike's

Image by Nikketa via Pixabay

5. Domino's Pizza

Pizza

Domino's Pizza was founded in Michigan, United States. It is perfect for quick and tasty pizzas. It has lots of flavor with sufficient amount of toppings. The best pizza here is the ExtravaganZZA pizza which is filled with pepperoni, ham, italian sausage, beef, onions, green peppers, mushrooms, and pretty much everything!

Find the nearest Domino's Pizza

Image by Mari via Pinterest

6. Jolibee

Chicken

Jolibee's first franchised outlet is first opened in Manila, the Philippines. Jolibee won the hearts of the filipinos because of its distinct taste of fried chicken which separates them from other franchises such as McDonalds and KFC. If you are not ordering Chickenjoy in Jolibee, then you are absolutely missing out this treasure!

Find the nearest Jolibee

Photo: Little Caesars

7. Little Caesars

Pizza

Little Caesars originated from Michigan, United States. It is one of the cheapest pizzas in the US and have fresh and delicious toppings to fill you up. Their best pizza is definitely the ExtraMostBestest Pizza filled with meat and cheese for all you meat-lovers!

Find the nearest Little Caesars

8. In-N-Out

Burger

In-N-Out is first started in California, United States. Their meat are fresh and never frozen to deliver only the best quality of patties to their customers. The best burger you should at least try once is the In-N-Out BUrger's Double-Double because if one patty from In-N-Out is never enough!

Find the nearest In-N-Out

Photo: Taco Bell

9. Taco Bell

Mixed

Taco Bell originated from California, United States. It is popular for its flavourful and juicy tacos. Among their best items are the Cheesy Gordita Crunch, the XXL Grilled Stuffed Burrito, and the Hard Taco.

Find the nearest Taco Bell

10. Chick-fil-A

Chicken

Chick-fil-A was founded in Georgia, United States. Their meat tastes great because they are good quality and organic chicken. Their number one item in their menu is definitely the basic Chicken Sandwich and fries! It's so good it does not need any additional flavours to it!
